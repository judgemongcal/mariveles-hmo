🏥 Services

Explore the diverse range of services and programs provided by our municipal health office.

Medical Consultation and Treatment

Provided at the 3 main health centers, with integrated management of childhood illnesses available in all barangay health stations. Barangay visits are conducted as scheduled.


Emergency Consultation and Treatment

Available at Mariveles Paramedics in Municipal Compound and Roman High-way in Townsite 24/7. Cases needing hospitalization are referred to higher medical facilities for further treatment and management through ambulance conduction.


Dental Health Services

Delivered at the 3 main health centers. Barangay visits are conducted as scheduled.


Maternal Health and Child Care Services

Prenatal checkups at BHSs and Main Health Centers, with Bemonc/Birthing Home available. Schedules may be adjusted whenever necessary.


Well Baby Clinics

Conducted in main health centers as needed.


Expanded Program on Immunization (EPI)

Includes immunization sessions, with adult vaccines such as FLU and Pneumonia given based on availability and schedule set by the coordinator.


Occupational Therapy - Available Monday to Friday by appointment.

Speech Therapy - Available Monday to Friday by appointment.

Child Psychological Evaluation - By appointment.

Rehabilitation Consultation - Available on Tuesdays by appointment.

National Tuberculosis Program - Includes screening for TB diseases in children, Multidrug-resistant tuberculosis, leprosy, and HIV-TB collaboration.

Control of Vaccine Prevention Diseases - Health program to combat outbreaks or prevent disease occurrence through immunization.

Malnutrition Program - Aimed at combating malnutrition, especially in vulnerable groups, and rehabilitating undernourished children. Programs include supplemental feeding (nutrition month) and lectures on proper nutrition.

Bantay Buntis Program - Ensuring safe motherhood until delivery.

Family Planning - Implementation of the reproductive health law.

Inspection of Establishments - Conducted Monday to Friday.

Issuance of Sanitary Permits - Available Mondays to Fridays.

Water Testing for Water Refilling Stations - Conducted on Mondays.

Water Testing for Other Establishments - Quarterly as scheduled.

Vector Surveillance - Scheduled as needed.

Fogging/Residual Spraying - Conducted as the need arises.

Salt Testing - Included in general practice and scheduled randomly.

Disposal of Medical Wastes and Hazardous Materials - Conducted monthly or as scheduled.

👥 About Us

Mission

The Municipal Health Office is tasked in the promotion and maintenance of health; and prevention of diseases through the provision of effective, relevant and efficient health services by well-trained, dedicated health personnel working hand-in-hand with the communities.

Vision

To provide quality Primary Health Care Services in the municipality of Mariveles, Bataan with adequate, dedicated and highly professional health services providers serving in functional, accessible and upgraded health facilities fully supported by the government and other sectors working together with empowered communities.
