Healthcare Services Offered

Medical Consultation (Non-Emergency) and Treatment

Available at the 3 main health centers Mondays thru Fridays from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM except during holidays; Integrated Management of Childhood Illnesses are available in all barangay health stations (BHSs) Mondays thru Fridays except during holidays. Barangay visits are conducted as scheduled.

Emergency Consultation and Treatment (non-complicated)

Available at Mariveles Paramedics in Municipal Compound and Roman High-way in Townsite 24 hours a day 7 days a week. Cases needing hospitalization are referred to higher medical facilities for further treatment and management thru ambulance conduction

Dental Health Services

Available at the 3 main health centers Mondays thru Fridays except during holidays. Barangay visits are conducted as scheduled.

Maternal Health and Child Care Services

Prenatal checkups every Thursday at BHSs and Main Health Centers and Bemonc/Birthing Home. Schedules may be adjusted whenever necessary.

Well Baby Clinics

Prenatal Conducted in main health centers every Wednesday or may be adjusted as need arises.

Expanded Program on Immunization (EPI)

Usually every Wednesday at main health centers. May be adjusted depending on the availability of vaccines, midwife, and nurse.

Adult vaccines such as FLU and Pneumonia vaccines are given by EPI coordinators depending on the availability of the Vaccines at a schedule set by the coordinator.

Rehabilitation Services

Medical Consultation (Non-Emergency) and Treatment - Monday thru Fridays (by appointment)

Occupational Therapy - Monday thru Fridays (by appointment)

Speech Therapy - Monday thru Fridays (by appointment)

Child Psychological Evaluation - By appointment

Rehabilitation Consultation - Tuesdays (by appointment)

Special Programs/Events

National Tuberculosis - The program includes TB diseases in children screening, Multidrug resistant tuberculosis, leprosy, and HIV-TB collaboration

Control of Vaccine Prevention Diseases - Health program to combat outbreaks or prevent occurrence of disease before it sets in through immunization

Malnutrition Program - To combat malnutrition especially vulnerable groups and to rehabilitate the undernourished child. Usual program include supplemental feeding (nutrition month) and lecturers on proper nutrition

Bantay Buntis Program - To ensure safe motherhood until delivery of the child

Family Planning - To implement the reproductive health law

Environmental Health and Sanitation Services

Inspection of Establishments - Monday thru Fridays

Issuance of Sanitary Permits - Mondays thru Fridays

Water Testing for Water refilling stations - Mondays

Water Testing for other Establishment - Quarterly as scheduled

Vector Surveillance - As scheduled

Fogging/Residual Spraying - As need arises

Salt Testing - Included in GP and scheduled in random

Disposal of Medical Wastes and Hazardous Materials - Monthly or as scheduled

👥 About Us

🎯

Mission

The Municipal Health Office is tasked in the promotion and maintenance of health; and prevention of diseases through the provision of effective, relevant and efficient health services by well-trained, dedicated health personnel working hand-in-hand with the communities.

👁

Vision

To provide quality Primary Health Care Services in the municipality of Mariveles, Bataan with adequate, dedicated and highly professional health services providers serving in functional, accessible and upgraded health facilities fully supported by the government and other sectors working together with empowered communities.
